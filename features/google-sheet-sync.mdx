title: "Google Sheets Sync"
description: "Sync your form responses directly to your Google Sheets"
---

Automatically send form submissions to your connected Google Sheet in real-time.

---

##  How to Connect

1. Click on **Connect Google Account** button
2. Grant permission to connect Google Sheets

<img style={{borderRadius: '0.5rem'}} src="/images/google-auth-connect.png"/>

---

##  What Gets Synced?

Each submission creates a new row in the sheet with:

- Field labels as column headers
- Responses as values


<img style={{borderRadius: '0.5rem'}} src="/images/google-sheet-output.png"/>

---
