# Notion Integration: A Notionmonkey Alternative

Heysheet can help you streamline your workflows by integrating with Notion. If you're looking for a flexible **Notionmonkey alternative** to manage your form submissions, Heysheet provides a powerful way to get your data into your Notion databases.

## How to Connect Notion

1.  **Connect Your Notion Workspace:**
    *   Navigate to your Heysheet Dashboard.
    *   Click on "Create Form" and connect your notion account. This is a one-time setup.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/create-form-btn.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

    * Now click on the "Notion" tab in the modal that appears and click on the "Connect Notion" button.
   
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/connect-to-notion.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>


    

2.  **Link a Form to a Notion Database:**
    *   Create a new form or select an existing one.
    *   Select the target Notion database where you want your form submissions to appear.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/notion-db-picker.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

## Key Features

*   **Automated Data Entry:** Form submissions automatically create new items in your Notion database.
*   **Customizable Mapping:** Map form fields to specific properties in your Notion database.
*   **Efficient Workflows:** Trigger Notion automations based on new form submissions.
