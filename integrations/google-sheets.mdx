# Google Sheets Integration: A Sheetmonkey Alternative

Heysheet provides seamless integration with Google Sheets, allowing you to collect form submissions directly into your spreadsheets in real-time. If you're looking for a powerful **Sheetmonkey alternative**, Heysheet offers a straightforward and efficient way to manage your data.

## How to Connect Google Sheets

1.  **Connect Your Google Account:**
    *   Navigate to your Heysheet Dashboard.
    *   Click on "Create Form" and grant Heysheet the necessary permissions to access your Google Sheets. This is a one-time setup.
 <Frame>
  <img src="/images/create-form-btn.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

    * Now click on the "Google Sheets" tab in the modal that appears and click on the "Connect Google Sheets" button.
   
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/connect-to-google-sheets.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>



2.  **Link a Form to a Spreadsheet:**
    *   Create a new form or select an existing one.
    *   Select an existing Google Sheet or create a new one directly from Heysheet.

  <img src="/images/google-sheet-picker.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />

## Key Features

*   **Real-time Sync:** Form submissions are instantly added to your connected Google Sheet.
*   **No Coding Required:** Set up your integration without writing any code.
*   **Data Ownership:** Your data resides entirely within your Google Sheets, giving you full control and transparency.
*   **Flexible Data Management:** Utilize all of Google Sheets' powerful features for analysis, sharing, and automation.
